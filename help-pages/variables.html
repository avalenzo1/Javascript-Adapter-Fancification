Variables

Home » Unit 1 » Variables



Variables can be used to replace a number. Variables must have a name that:

  • Begins with a lowercase letter; Capitalization matters!
  • No spaces!

    • Good Examples of variables:

  • a
  • b12
  • pumpkinPie

    • Bad examples of variables:

  • 8a
  • blueberry Pie
